PebbleSMS Config

Heads up! Please note when entering your contacts names and numbers, you have to enter the number in a special format. Ask your contacts their cell carriers and enter their numbers using the format below.

  • Rogers

    10digitcontactsnumber@pcs.rogers.com. Please note a few applies to those you text on Rogers.

  • Telus

    0digitcontactsnumber@msg.telus.com. Please note they will receive your message from Telus Email to SMS service. There is no fee.

  • Bell

    0digitcontactsnumber@txt.bell.ca. Please note they will get your message as yournumber@msg.telus.com. There is no fee.

  • TextNow

    username@textnow.me. Please note they will get your message as yournumber@msg.telus.com. There is no fee.
